Maze Memory Game

Welcome to Maze Game! A game that tests your photographic memory.

You will be shown a grid that represents a maze for 5 seconds. The brown squares are the walls, the entrance to the maze is red and the exit is green. You are free to walk on the white space. Directions are either North (N), South (S), East(E) or West(W). One N direction moves one square up, one E direction moves one square right and so on. Your task is to memorise the maze and input the directions in the box Some rules:
- If you hit a wall you are dead
- If you don't give enough instructions you will stay lost in the maze
- If you go outside the borders of the maze you will die
